Our Story

A decade of curating the world's finest products for those who appreciate the extraordinary.

Born from a passion for excellence

Luxe was founded in 2016 with a simple belief: that the objects we surround ourselves with should be as thoughtful and intentional as the lives we lead.

What began as a small atelier in New York's Lower East Side has grown into a global destination for discerning customers who refuse to compromise on quality, craftsmanship, or design.

Today, we partner with over 200 brands across 40 countries, each chosen for their unwavering commitment to excellence. Every product in our collection has been personally reviewed by our team of curators — artisans, designers, and connoisseurs who share our obsession with detail.

Our Values

What we stand for

Uncompromising Quality

We accept nothing less than exceptional. Every product must meet our rigorous standards for materials, craftsmanship, and design before it earns a place in our collection.

Sustainable Luxury

True luxury is responsible. We prioritize brands that practice ethical manufacturing, use sustainable materials, and minimize their environmental footprint.

Human Connection

Behind every product is a story — of artisans, families, and traditions. We celebrate the people who bring these objects to life and share their stories with you.

Return Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority. Here's everything you need to know about returns.

Last updated: July 19, 2026

30-Day Return Guarantee

At Luxe, we want you to love every purchase. If you're not completely satisfied with your order, you may return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • Unused and in the same condition that you received it
  • In the original packaging with all tags attached
  • Accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase

Non-Returnable Items

Certain items cannot be returned for hygiene and safety reasons:

  • Beauty and skincare products (once opened)
  • Fragrances (once opened)
  • Undergarments and swimwear
  • Final sale items marked as such

How to Initiate a Return

Log into your account, navigate to "My Orders," and select the item you wish to return. Follow the prompts to generate a prepaid return shipping label. Alternatively, contact our concierge team at returns@luxestore.com.

Refund Processing

Once we receive your return, we'll inspect the item and process your refund within 5–7 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
